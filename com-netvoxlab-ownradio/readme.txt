== Installation ==

Follow these instructions to install ownRadio plugin:

> 1. Download the plugin from the “Download” button above.
> 2. Upload the `com-netvoxlab-ownradio` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins` directory.
> 3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
> 4. Set the ownRadio shortcode in your posts and/or pages to display the radio player: `[ownradio_player]`

Alternatively, you can find our plugin through your WordPress dashboard:

> 1. Go to the Plugins section in your WordPress site's dashboard.
> 2. Click the "Add New" button.
> 3. Search for "ownRadio".
> 4. When found, click on the "Install" button, then hit the "Activate" button once it has installed.
> 5. Use the ownRadio shortcode in your posts and/or pages to display the radio player: `[ownradio_player]`

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
